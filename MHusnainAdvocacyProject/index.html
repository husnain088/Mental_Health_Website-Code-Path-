Why Mental Health is important?

Mental health is a challenge because people sometimes don't talk
about it and finding help can be tough.

The way we think and feel doesn't always get the attention it
needs in a world that doesn't fully understand or focus on emotional well-being.

These are some affects caused by mental health:

  • Emotional well being
  • Physical health
  • Cognitive Functioning
  • Social Interactions

Main goal:

The man goal of my topic is to spread awareness about, mental health and how it affects our lives.

A Comprehensive Look at Global Mental Health Statistics"

These are some statistics that shows the percentage of people who are suffering from mental health.

  1. Global Prevalence
    • Around 1 in 4 people suffer from mental illness (According to WHO)
  2. Specific Disorders:
    • Depression: Estimated to affect over 264 million people worldwide.
    • An estimated 3.8% of the global population is affected by anxiety disorders.
    • Bipolar Disorder: Approximately 1-2% of the global population has bipolar disorder.

Where can this problem have lasting effects?

Mentall health can cause serious problems Social life and perosonal relationship like.

  • It can cause strained family dynamics and challenges in maintaining healthy relationships.
  • Mental health can cause difficulty forming and sustaining connections with friends and partner.

How can we overcome?

There are so many ways to overcome mental health.

  1. Seek Professional Help:
    • Consult with a mental health professional, such as a psychologist, psychiatrist, or counselor, to receive expert guidance and tailored treatment.
  2. Build a Support System:
    • Share your feelings and experiences with trusted friends, family members, or support groups.
    • Cultivate a strong support network to provide encouragement and understanding.
  3. Connect with Nature:
    • Spend time outdoors and connect with nature, which can have positive effects on mental well-being.
